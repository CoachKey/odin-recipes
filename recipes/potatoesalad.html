Poatate Salad Recipe

Ingredients

  • Golden Potates
  • Pickles
  • Red inions
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • 3 Eggs
  • Your favorite Mayo
  • 1 cup of love :)

Recipe instructions

  1. Start your cooking out with Bismillah and love from your heart :)
  2. Cut up your potatoes in cubes.
  3. Boil your Potates.
  4. Cut up your red onions into tiny squares or however you prefer them.
  5. Cut up your pickles and place them to the side.
  6. When the potatoes are done, drain them and let them cool a bit.
  7. Throw your potatoes, onions, seasonings, and pickles in a bowl and mix.
  8. Put is in the fridge for an hour, plate and enjoy :)
