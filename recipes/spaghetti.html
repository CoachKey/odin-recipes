My Famous Spaghetti Recipe

Ingredients

  • ground turkey
  • spaghetti sauce
  • salt/pepper
  • diced garlic
  • diced onions
  • diced tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • 1 cup of love :)
  • spaghetti noodles

Recipe instructions (lol taken from my memory :))

  1. Start your cooking out with Bismillah and love from your heart :)
  2. Dice your tomatoes, garlic, onions and put them to the side for now.
  3. Boil your water for the noodles, add a little salt and olive oil to the water.
  4. Add your diced onions and garlic to the saucepan with a little olive oil and let it cook for a few minutes.
  5. Add in your ground turkey to the diced onions and let that cook for a few then add your diced tomatoes.
  6. Once your water comes to a boil, add your noodles and let them cook
  7. Add your tomato sauce and let that cook to the side.
  8. Once the turkey and sauce are done add them together and let it simmer.
  9. When everything is done, plate your noodles then add your turkey and sauce on top of the noodles and enjoy.
