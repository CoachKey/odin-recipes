Roasted Potatoes Recipe

Ingredients

  • Golden potatoes
  • Garlic Powder
  • salt/pepper
  • Paprika
  • Olive oil
  • 1 cup of love :)

Recipe instructions

  1. Start your cooking out with Bismillah and love from your heart :)
  2. Preheat the oven to about 400.
  3. Chop up your Golden potatoes to the size you prefer. The smaller the cut the faster they cook.
  4. Add a little of olive oil, garlic Powder, garlic inion, and paprika on top.
  5. Spread your potatoes out on your pan. I add aluminum foil so I don't have to do too much cleaning afterwards :).
  6. Let them cook for about 20-25 minutes or until they are as golden or crispy as you would like them.
  7. Plate and hopefully enjoy :)
